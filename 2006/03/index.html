  • Libraries as Playlists March 14, 2006 I love reading [Dave Weinberger's blog](http://www.hyperorg.com/blogger/); his excitement at the ways technology can augment the world mirrors my own. I love [this post](http://www.hyperorg.com/blogger/mtarchive/reading_and_libraries_two_note.html) for the reduction of where Web 2.0 and ubiquitous metadata are heading us: "When all our works are digitized, a local library will be nothing but a playlist." Think about that ... mash up [Google Books](http://books.google.com) with a [video iPod](http://www.apple.com/ipod/ipod.html) and **BAM** -- it's a library in your pocket. Toss in [Audible.com](http://www.audible.com/) and it's even better. I'm with Dave ... I can't _wait!_
