  • Reflections on moderated blog comments August 31, 2008 Ran into another blog today with misleading, subjective assertions about topical content and which moderates all comments. It's made me realize that, if you intend to blog about current events with any integrity, you absolutely must unmoderate comments and be ready to fight the spam. Otherwise, you've risk starting a monolog, not a conversation, and [the web has enough monologs](http://www.google.com/search?q=blog+monolog+dialog).
