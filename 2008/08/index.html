Posts from August 2008

  • Reflections on moderated blog comments August 31, 2008 Ran into another blog today with misleading, subjective assertions about topical content and which moderates all comments. It's made me realize that, if you intend to blog about current events with any integrity, you absolutely must unmoderate comments and be ready to fight the spam. Otherwise, you've risk starting a monolog, not a conversation, and [the web has enough monologs](http://www.google.com/search?q=blog+monolog+dialog).
  • Reports of the Business Card's Death Are Greatly Exaggerated, or why bzCards != biz cards August 20, 2008 Caught the article ["rmbr launches mobile app to get rid of business cards"](http://venturebeat.com/2008/08/19/rmbr-launches-a-mobile-app-to-get-rid-of-business-cards/) on VentureBeat which covers the announcement [rmbrME](http://rmbrme.com), the new electronic connection tool from [rmbr](http://blog.chroniql.com/). (note: the [rmbr.com](http://rmbr.com/) site, which [launched as a photo organizing site](http://www.readwriteweb.com/archives/rmbr_using_funware_to_organize_photos.php), currently redirects to [rmbrme.com](http://rmbrme.com/)) The general idea is to trade vCards over email, IM, SMS, etc. and get rid of those pesky piles of pasteboard. If the ease of use isn't incentive enough, they're mixing in the [funware](http://blogs.electricsheepcompany.com/swords/2008/02/18/worlds-in-motion-funware-is-the-future-of-our-virtual-world/) [idea](http://venturebeat.com/2008/05/09/funwares-threat-to-the-traditional-video-game-industry/) via contests and leaderboards.
